Research
for the website

The design of our website's home page is inspired by a reference website. We liked the way their navigation bar, background image, and footer were designed, so we decided to use similar elements in our own design. However, we also made some modifications to make it unique to our website. In the second picture, you can see how we implemented these changes and created our own version of the home page. We aimed to create a visually appealing and user-friendly design that captures the essence of the reference website while still maintaining our own identity.

Our product page design is inspired by a reference website that we found appealing. We liked how they arranged multiple products on their page, and we wanted to incorporate a similar layout on our website. After taking inspiration from that website, we made some modifications to make the design our own. In the second picture, you can see the result of these changes. We aimed to create a visually appealing and organized product page that showcases our products effectively. The arrangement of the products on the page is designed to make it easy for our customers to browse and find what they're looking for. We hope that this modified design enhances the user experience and helps our customers make informed decisions about the products we offer.

Our blog page design draws inspiration from a reference website that we found appealing. We liked the way they designed their blog page and wanted to create a similar layout on our own website. After taking inspiration from that website, we made some modifications to make the design unique to our brand. In the second picture, you can see the result of these changes. Our aim was to create an attractive and user-friendly blog page that effectively presents our blog posts. The layout is designed to make it easy for visitors to navigate and read the blog content. We hope that this modified design enhances the overall user experience and encourages our readers to engage with our blog articles.
